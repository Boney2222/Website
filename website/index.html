the shop

A desk-lover's corner shop

PAN LATE PYAR started as a single shelf of imported notebooks in 2021. Since then, we still test every pen ourselves, stock small local makers alongside old favourites, and believe a good notebook should outlast the year printed on its cover.

Whether you're five and picking your first sticker sheet, or fifty and hunting for the perfect fountain pen nib — there's a shelf for you here.
